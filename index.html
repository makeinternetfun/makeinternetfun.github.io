Manifesto

We need to make the Internet a fun place again.

The quirky, the kooky, the ugly, the imperfect.

The creative, the mind-bending, the laugh out loud place it used to be.

No more autonomous doom scrolling. No more click-baiting.

No more problem solving. We've solved all the important problems that can be addressed digitally.

Now we're just running up the scoreboard. Solving micro problems.

Time to take your life back.

Reclaim the joy of living.
