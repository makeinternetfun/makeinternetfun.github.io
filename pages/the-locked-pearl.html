The Locked Pearl

Concept

A dream journal site where users can record the dreams they had while sleeping.

Optionally, they'll be able to generate photos or videos based on the descriptions and other characteristics of the dreams.

I'd like to try and do some other wild stuff like be able to 'stitch' together multiple dreams into a single narrative, or generate a 'dream map' that shows how different dreams are related to each other.

This is a real project and the very primative beta version is basically done, so it should be linked here in the coming weeks.
